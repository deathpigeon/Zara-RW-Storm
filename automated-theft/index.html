Auto(mated) Theft

Zara RW Storm

In the near future in Siberia, coal mining has been completely automated, including shipping which is done by self-driving trucks filled with coal, destroying local ecosystems and communities, who see none of the benefits.

These automated systems are exploited by a team of bandit-hackers to hijack the trucks and steal the coal as the company takes measures to stop them.

It's a war in Siberia between bandit-hackers and a coal company.
